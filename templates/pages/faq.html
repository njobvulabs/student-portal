Account Management

Click the "Register" button in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. Make sure to provide accurate information, especially if you're registering as a student.

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Course Management

Browse available courses in the Courses section. Click on a course to view details, then click the "Enroll" button. Some courses may require instructor approval.

After enrolling in a course, you can access course materials from your dashboard or the course page. Materials may include lectures, assignments, and resources.

Grades and Progress

Access your grades through the Grades section in your dashboard. You can view individual assignment grades and overall course grades.

Your progress is tracked automatically as you complete assignments and assessments. View your progress bars and statistics in your dashboard.

Technical Support

Contact our support team through the Contact page or email support@studentportal.com. Include details about the issue and any error messages you see.

Our platform works best with modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Make sure to keep your browser updated for the best experience.
